# Recommended Books

## Fundamentals
- Russell & Norvig. Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach (4th ed.)
- Mitchell. Machine Learning.
- Bishop. Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning.

## Deep Learning
- Goodfellow, Bengio, Courville. Deep Learning. (free online)
- Zhang et al. Dive into Deep Learning. (free online)

## NLP
- Jurafsky & Martin. Speech and Language Processing.
- Eisenberg. Natural Language Processing.

## Reinforcement Learning
- Sutton & Barto. Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction (2nd ed.)

## MLOps
- Huyen. Designing Machine Learning Systems.
- Lakshmanan et al. Machine Learning Design Patterns.

## Ethics
- Bostrom. Superintelligence.
- Russell. Human Compatible.
